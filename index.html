JOHN BOELEE

John Boelee – Contemporary Artist

Based in Spain and originally from the Netherlands, my practice moves between abstract painting, figurative elements, realism, and mixed media, exploring the tension between perception, memory, and lived experience.

Working with layered imagery, gesture, and collected visual material, my work investigates how images and memories shift over time. Fragments of observation, personal history, and visual culture overlap to form open compositions that resist fixed meaning.

Through this approach, the work becomes a reflection on how we construct reality — not as something stable, but as something constantly evolving.
