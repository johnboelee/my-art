JOHN BOELEE
CONTEMPORARY ART

I am drawn to the moment when certainty begins to dissolve. The work exists in the tension between what we know, what we see, and what we experience.

Images, memories, and observations enter the practice as fragments that drift, overlap, and quietly contradict one another.

Whether structured through collected imagery or developed through gesture and abstraction, each work becomes a space where multiple realities can coexist.
