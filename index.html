JOHN BOELEE

Based in Spain and originally from the Netherlands, my work moves between abstraction and representation. Through painting, drawing and mixed media, I explore the tension between memory and perception, and the fragile space between what an image preserves and what it inevitably loses. Rather than offering fixed meanings, the works remain open to multiple readings. I am interested in how images change over time—through making, remembering and looking—and how each encounter subtly transforms what is seen.
