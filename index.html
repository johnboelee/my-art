JOHN BOELEE

Based in Spain and originally from the Netherlands, my work moves between abstraction and representation. Through layered imagery and mixed media, I explore the shifting nature of memory, perception, and experience. Each piece invites multiple interpretations, reflecting reality as something fluid, subjective, and constantly evolving.
