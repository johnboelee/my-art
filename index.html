JOHN BOELEE
CONTEMPORARY ART

I am drawn to the moment when certainty begins to dissolve. The work moves within the tension between what we know, what we see, and what we experience.

Images, memories, and observations gather in my practice, where fragments of meaning drift, overlap, and quietly contradict one another.

Whether structured through collected imagery or developed through gesture and abstraction, each artwork becomes a space in which different realities can exist simultaneously.

LION WATCHING BACK · 1993

LAYERS OF ABSENCE · 2025
