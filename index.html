JOHN BOELEE
CONTEMPORARY ART
I am interested in the moment when certainty begins to shift. My work explores the tension between what we know, what we see and what we experience. Images, memories and observations are brought together through painting, where fragments of meaning can coexist, overlap and sometimes contradict one another. This state of ambiguity interests me. Rather than resolving contradictions, the work often embraces them. Whether structured through collected imagery or developed through gesture and abstraction, each painting becomes a space in which different realities can exist simultaneously.
Lion Watching Back 1993
Contemporary Art Practice
Based in The Netherlands
